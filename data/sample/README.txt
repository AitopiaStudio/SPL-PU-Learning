Fair For You Credit Dataset
============================
Source: Fair For You (UK community lender) — used in UCL MSc dissertation, 2022.

Files:
  all_data.csv    — Full dataset (1000 samples, 149 features, label: "Approved")
  train_data.csv  — Training split (800 samples)
  test_data.csv   — Test split (200 samples)

Class distribution:
  Positive (Approved=1): 810  (81%)
  Negative (Approved=0): 190  (19%)

Label column: "Approved"
Used as dataset name: "exp" in the codebase (see utils.py)
